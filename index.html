Our Story, Reimagined

Nestled in the heart of Hanmer Springs, Tease has always been more than just a place to eat and drink. It’s a place where good food and good company come together. Now, we’ve infused this tradition with a vision of the future.

Introducing Hanmer Springs’ first robotic dining service. Our state-of-the-art Cadebot waiters and Robot Anno barista work in harmony with our human team to provide an experience that is both flawlessly efficient and wonderfully unique. We blend the best of Asian and Kiwi flavours, creating a menu that is both familiar and exciting.

Join us for a taste of tomorrow, today.
